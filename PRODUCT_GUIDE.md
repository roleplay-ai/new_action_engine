# Nudgeable Action Engine: Product & Feature Guide

## 🎯 Overview
The **Nudgeable Action Engine** is a behavioral science platform designed to solve the "Knowing-Doing Gap"—the distance between understanding a concept and actually applying it in the workplace. It shifts the focus from passive learning to active, repeatable micro-behaviors.

---

## 🚀 Core User Features

### 1. The Action Library (Challenges)
Users browse a curated "Deck" of micro-actions categorized by strategic themes (e.g., *Accountability, Feedback, Collaboration*).
*   **Search & Filter**: Quickly find actions by status (Active, Completed) or theme.
*   **Action Details**: Each card explains the **How** (tactical steps) and the **Why** (cognitive impact).

### 2. Intention & Planning
An action is only effective if it has a specific trigger. 
*   **The Plan Overlay**: Users commit to a specific day and time.
*   **Calendar Sync**: Optional integration that doubles the XP reward for increasing commitment strength.

### 3. Validation Queue
Once an action's scheduled time has passed, it enters the Validation Queue.
*   **Verify Impact**: Users must confirm if they actually performed the action.
*   **Impact Notes**: An optional reflection field to document the qualitative results of the nudge.

### 4. The Habit Loop (Repetition Engine)
The engine identifies actions that shouldn't be one-offs.
*   **Rep Loops**: Upon successful validation, users can be prompted to start a "Habit Loop."
*   **Cementing**: An action must be successfully validated 5 times (configurable) before it is marked as "Cemented"—turning a behavior into a permanent trait.

## 📊 Behavioral Analytics

### The Engagement Funnel
The platform tracks users across four distinct behavioral phases:
1.  **Knowledge**: The user has read the action.
2.  **Intention**: The user has scheduled the action.
3.  **Action**: The user has successfully validated the first completion.
4.  **Habit**: The user has completed the full "Rep Loop."

### The Effort Ledger
A transparent breakdown of how "Behavioral Points" (XP) are earned:
*   **Acceptance**: 3 XP
*   **Honesty Skip**: 1 XP (Encourages engagement even when not acting)
*   **Successful Validation**: 5 XP
*   **Habit Cementing**: 10 XP
*   **Streaks**: Points are multiplied by the user's weekly consistency.

## 👥 Social & Team Dynamics

### The Nudgeboard
A real-time feed of team activity designed to build social proof and collective momentum.
*   **Live Broadcast**: See when teammates commit to or "crush" their nudges.
*   **Interactions**: Users can "Like" activities to encourage their peers.
*   **Contextual Statuses**: Unique badges for "Win" (Success), "Intent" (Scheduled), and "Mastered" (Cemented).

### Leaderboards & Leagues
Users are placed into Leagues (*Starter, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond*) based on their total XP. This drives friendly competition centered around **effort**, not just output.

## 🛠️ Admin & Architect Suite

Designed for HRBP’s and Team Leads to manage organizational change at scale.

### 1. Analyze Change Dashboard
A high-level view of organizational health:
*   **Adoption Index**: Identifies which behaviors are being embraced and which are meeting resistance.
*   **Global Funnel**: Tracks the aggregate "leakage" where users drop off from Knowledge to Habit.
*   **Skill Drivers**: Visualizes organizational growth in areas like *Learning Agility* or *Trust*.

### 2. The Control Panel (Architect Wizard)
A 3-step tool for deploying behavioral campaigns:
1.  **Architect Content**: Define the skill focus and select actions from the master bank.
2.  **Pulse Logic**: Set the duration (e.g., 8 weeks) and frequency of nudges.
3.  **Deploy & Enrol**: Upload target recipient lists and set the "Rule of 5" for habit loops.

### 3. Custom Action Architect
Allows admins to create their own micro-actions tailored to specific company values or unique project needs.

## 🎨 Visual Language: Neo-Brutalism
The platform uses a "Neo-Brutalist" design system to emphasize **clarity** and **directness**:
*   **High Contrast**: Bold black borders (`#000`) and vibrant accents (`#FFCE00`, `#3699FC`).
*   **No Fluff**: Minimalist shadows and direct typography to reduce cognitive load.
*   **Tactile UI**: Elements feel "physical" to mimic the real-world actions they represent.
